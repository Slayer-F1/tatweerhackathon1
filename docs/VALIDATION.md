# Community validation script & template

Criterion 6 rewards claims **backed by community validation**. This is the highest-leverage, human-only task — even **3–5 real responses** move you from "asserted" to "validated". Send these on WhatsApp to Al Qua'a / Al Ain residents, camel-farm owners, and people who've done a desert stargazing trip. Paste real answers into README §9.

## The 5 questions (EN)

1. When you plan a desert/stargazing trip, **how do you currently decide which night to go?** (Do you check the moon or weather at all?)
2. Have you ever **driven out and been disappointed** by clouds or a bright moon? What happened?
3. If an app told you **"tonight scores 88/100 — clear, no moon, dark from 8:40pm"**, would you use it? Why / why not?
4. *(For farm owners)* Would you **host stargazing visitors** on your farm for income? What would you need to feel comfortable doing it?
5. What would make you **trust** a booking like this (vs. a global app)?

## الأسئلة الخمسة (AR)

1. عندما تخطط لرحلة رصد نجوم في الصحراء، **كيف تقرر حاليًا أي ليلة تذهب؟** (هل تتحقق من القمر أو الطقس؟)
2. هل سبق أن **ذهبت وخاب أملك** بسبب الغيوم أو القمر الساطع؟ ماذا حدث؟
3. لو أخبرك تطبيق أن **«الليلة 88/100 — صافية، بلا قمر، الظلام من 8:40م»**، هل ستستخدمه؟ ولماذا؟
4. *(لأصحاب المزارع)* هل تستضيف زوار الرصد الفلكي في مزرعتك مقابل دخل؟ ما الذي تحتاجه لتطمئن لذلك؟
5. ما الذي يجعلك **تثق** بحجز كهذا (مقابل تطبيق عالمي)؟

## How to log a response (paste into README §9)

```
- "<their quote>" — <first name / role>, <Al Ain / Al Qua'a>, <date>
```

## Quick quantitative check (optional, strong)

Ask 10 people Q3 (yes/no). Reporting **"8/10 said they'd use it"** is a concrete, falsifiable validation metric judges love.
